常见问题 Frequently Asked Questions

如何领取离线收益？ How do I claim offline rewards?

通过升级和解锁牧场区与流水线，即可让奶牛在您离线期间自动生产牛奶，从而持续赚取 DIAMOND。額外的 DIAMOND 獎勵將會在用戶達到某些任務是獲得。

Upgrade and unlock ranch areas and production lines to allow cows to produce milk while you're offline, continuously earning DIAMOND. Bonus DIAMOND rewards can also be obtained upon completing specific tasks.

如何获得更多 DIAMOND？ How to earn more DIAMOND?
您可以通过以下方式获得 DIAMOND：
  • 完成任务系统中的各类任务
  • 参与推广活动，邀请新用户，获取更多宝箱奖励，宝箱中有机会开出 DIAMOND 和其他丰厚奖励。
You can earn DIAMOND through the following methods:
  • Complete various missions in the task system
  • Participate in referral events and invite new users to earn chest rewards. Chests may contain DIAMOND and other valuable prizes.
推广奖励规则是怎样的？ How does the referral reward work?
每成功邀请一位新用户：
  • 您将获得一次宝箱奖励
  • 同时系统将该用户标记为您的“下线”，为后续更多推广收益打下基础
For each successful referral:
  • You will receive a chest reward
  • The system will mark the invited user as your “downline,” laying the foundation for more future referral earnings
