常见问题 Frequently Asked Questions

如何领取离线收益？ How do I claim offline rewards?

离线收益会自动累积。在第一版游戏中，离线收益分为两部分：

  1. 倒计时宝箱：每24小时刷新一次，需等待倒计时结束后开启，内含离线奖励。
  2. 农场GEM收益：在您离线期间，系统会根据农场产出自动累积GEM奖励。

您可以在主页点击“收益中心”查看并领取当前可得的离线收益。

Offline rewards accumulate automatically. In the first version, they consist of:

  1. Countdown Chest: Refreshes every 24 hours. Once ready, open it for offline rewards.
  2. Farm GEM Earnings: While offline, your farm continues generating GEMs automatically.

You can view and claim these rewards in the “Earnings Center” on the homepage.

宝箱奖励如何获取？ How do I get treasure chests?
宝箱奖励可通过多种方式获取：完成每日任务、参与社区活动、邀请好友、倒计时宝箱等。不同类型的宝箱包含不同稀有度的奖励，Jackpot宝箱有机会获得游戏中最稀有的物品。
You can get treasure chests through daily tasks, community events, referrals, and countdown timers. Different chests offer varying rarities, and Jackpot chests contain the rarest items in the game.
推广奖励规则？ How does the referral reward work?

成功邀请一位新用户后，您将获得一次宝箱奖励。

此外，您的下线好友的 倒计时宝箱将自动获得加速，更快开启宝箱。

Successfully invite a new player to receive a free chest reward.

Also, your downline’s countdown chests will open faster thanks to your boost.
